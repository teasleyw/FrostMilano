🧊 THE LEGEND 🧊

^ actual unretouched photo ^

FROST MILANO is two things at once. On record: hip-hop. On stage: a guitar, an amp, and the blues.

Born in Denver, raised in Rochester Hills, just outside Detroit. Didn't pick up an instrument or write a song until after college.

Six months of jamming around Austin since then — sitting in wherever there was room, taking over when a friend needed a cigarette break.

Now there's a band. DIRTY WHISKEY plays blues and classic rock. July 30 at Valhalla is the first show.
